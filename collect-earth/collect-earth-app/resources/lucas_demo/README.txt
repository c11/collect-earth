ABOUT THIS DEMO:
The purpose of this demo is to show how Collect Earth can be used in a Land Use assessment.



LUCAS :
LUCAS is a survey conducted in the European Union to produce Land Use/Cover data.
Please check this website for more information: 
http://epp.eurostat.ec.europa.eu/portal/page/portal/lucas/introduction

The classification scheme used in this demo is a copy of that used in the Land Use classification used in LUCAS 2013 LUCAS SU LU (LUCAS Survey Land Use) : http://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/ramon/other_documents/lucas/index.htm

Classification schema: http://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/ramon/other_documents/lucas/LUCAS2012_20131003.xlsx

Instructions for surveyors : http://epp.eurostat.ec.europa.eu/portal/page/portal/lucas/documents/LUCAS2009_C3-Classification_20121004.pdf



DISCLAIMER:
FAO is not related in any way to the European Union LUCAS Survey.
This software is produced only for demo purposes. Collect Earth is not connected to the European Commission and WAS NOT used for the LUCAS assessment and does not claim any connection to it.

Spain was selected for the grid creation just to show the possiblities of COllect Earth in a varied country.
